About South Texas Bank

Serving our community with integrity, innovation, and personal service since 1985.

Our Mission

South Texas Bank was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional banking services that help our neighbors, businesses, and communities thrive. We believe that banking should be personal, accessible, and focused on building lasting relationships.

As a locally-owned institution, we understand the unique needs of South Texas. Our decisions are made locally, our profits stay in the community, and our commitment to your success is unwavering.
